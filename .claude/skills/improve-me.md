name: improve-me
description: Reads the codebase and critically evaluates design choices and implementations. Use when user wants to improve a code-base, get questioned on their design choices, or mentions "improve me".
---

Read the codebase that I have written. Go through each line. Ask me questions about why I made different choices in design, dependenices and implementations.
Suggest your recommendations and evaluate how the suggestion will make the codebase better. Be critical. Prioritize simplicity over complexity. 

Take one Issue at a time. 

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
